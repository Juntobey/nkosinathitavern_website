About Us

Nkosinathi Tavern has been a big part of our community since we opened in 2014. It's not just a place to get a drink;
it's where friends, neighbors, and folks from the area come to chill, hang out, and catch up.

Customer Focus

Nkosinathi Tavern is a confidently managed family business, dedicated to fostering a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere.
With almost a decade of serving the community, the owner brings 14 years of industry experience; She brings her passion for hospitality
and her knowledge of the business to create a place where everyone feels comfortable.

At Nkosinathi Tavern, the family-centric approach is evident in every aspect of the business.
Even deliveries are handled by the owner's children, instilling a strong sense of community pride and family values.
This commitment to a familial atmosphere extends to our customers, ensuring that every visit is met with a warm and genuine welcome.

Our Customers

Nkosinathi Tavern welcomes a diverse crowd of people who all share a love for community and good company.
Our prime location next to the Shumela Soccer Stadium, which opened in 2022,
means we get plenty of fans stopping by before or after games to chat about the match and enjoy a celebratory drink.

We extend a warm welcome to those in search of a tranquil setting to relax after a long week.
Friends, and neighbors gather to socialize and engage in a game of pool
